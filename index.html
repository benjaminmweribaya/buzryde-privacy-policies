BuzRyde Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025

1. Introduction

BuzRyde is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide through our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email, and phone number, when you sign up.
  • Location Data: required for ride tracking and matching drivers with passengers.
  • Device Information: including device type, operating system, and app version.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate with you about your rides and account
  • Ensure your safety and compliance with local regulations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with third-party service providers for operational purposes (e.g., payment processing), but only to the extent necessary.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
